Designing Website Pcari Channel

During my internship, I contributed to designing the Pcari Channel, a platform dedicated to delivering news in a modern and accessible format. The website provides users with a streamlined interface to explore trending stories, stay informed on global events, and enjoy a personalized reading experience.

Role and Contribution

I focused on enhancing the readability and usability of the platform for diverse audiences, ensuring the design was visually engaging and intuitive to navigate.

Challenges

The biggest challenge I faced was deciding what to include in the Pcari Channel. My boss provided no specific requirements or guidance, only instructing me to "create a news platform." Through self-reliance and creativity, I ensured the platform met the expectations of a professional news site without external guidance.
